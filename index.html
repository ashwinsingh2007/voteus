E-CELL Student Portal


This is the E-CELL Portal for student of LNCT to make them participate in our E-CELL Panel indirectly.

This portal is designed to participate student in our Associate Members Panel without being a member of our E-CELL Team.

Students can participate on our decisions and can tell their suggestions provided on the portal and can have the benefits of members of E-CELL virtually.
This portal is just a part of our E-CELL website which will keep a transparency between E-Cell and general students.The student can participate in each and every event of E-CELL through it virtually.
A new topic will be shared on day to day basis on this portal.
Students/Lnctians are requested to vote their choices
The most active member of our E-CEll Voting Portal will be honoured as "Most Active Student-ECELL" by our E-CELL.
